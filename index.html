What I do

Three kinds of work founders hire me for.

Sometimes it starts with a conversation. Sometimes it is the whole engagement.

01

Think it through with you

The high-stakes call, the strategic decision, the founder-only problem. We find the real shape of the problem, name your trade-offs and spot the levers available. You leave with a clearer head and a sharp decision.

02

Lead the cross-functional project

The work that doesn't sit cleanly with anyone on the team and keeps slipping. I shift gears and deliver: leading the project, running the workstream, or shipping the system, with your team in the loop and you out of the weeds.

03

Redesign the operations layer

Workflows, handovers, CRMs, tooling. I take the manual processes quietly costing you time and revenue, map them, and replace them with automated, AI-assisted systems built to scale.
